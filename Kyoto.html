About Kyoto

Kyoto, the former imperial capital of Japan, is renowned for its well-preserved temples, traditional wooden houses, and beautiful gardens. It is a city that reflects the rich cultural heritage of Japan.

Explore historic sites such as Kinkaku-ji (Golden Pavilion), Fushimi Inari Taisha, and Arashiyama Bamboo Grove. Kyoto offers a glimpse into Japan's ancient past and is a must-visit for cultural enthusiasts.
